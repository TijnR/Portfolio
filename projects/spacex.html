PROJECTS

PROJECT TYPE: FRONT-END(SOLO)

TASKS: DESIGN + CREATE

For this assignment I had to create three different pages for spaceX. Each page focuses on something different. The first page I have to use chart.js to display charts on my website. The second page focuses on working with different APIs and the last page was the assignment to make a clock. I decided to take up the challenge and add an analog clock. I really liked working on this website, because I also find space very interesting, but also that I had to make three very different pages and combine them together.

PROCESS

INSPIRATION

I really wanted to use sci fi style for this website. I went looking for different images that have this style and continued with this. I wanted to keep the style across all pages. My goal was not to make the site too boring, but clear.

BUILD

SKETCHES

I started making sketches. During the process I kept in touch with a friend who is studying Physics and Astronomy to give some kind of realistic data. At the trip page you can add your weight and age and see how this is going. For the landing page I give a score that consists of different data that the page gets from the api. On the clock page you see two different clocks where you can also see whether it is day or night.
