HCI PROJECTS

Wearables

For this challenge, the intention is to create a handy wearable with arduino. The Arduino board is an open source micro controller that you can use in many different ways.

RESEARCH

Nadi X

NADI X is een smart yoga pants. Deze wearable kost maar liefst $250 en is dus ook voor een specifieke doelgroep. Deze wearable werkt met verschillende sensoren die met elkaar samenwerken om perfectie feedback te geven wanneer je met oefeningen bezig bent. Het gebruikt trillingen die directioneel aanvoelen, waardoor jij jouw form kan verbeteren.

TINKERING

Demo's

For this vase we have to create different demos with an Arduinoboard. I find it very cool that I can bring code to life and use it in the real world. For the demos I use different sensors.

Demo 1 - Speedup

For this demo you can adjust the speed of the flashing light by turning the potentiometer. also the pitch will rise as the light flashes faster.

Demo 2 - Motor

For the second demo I use the potentiometer again. Now when turning, the gear of the mini servo will turn.

Demo 3 - Controller

For the third challenge I made a controller to navigate the 8x8 matrix. I do this by using four buttons that all have their own direction. In arduino I have created x and y variables that change when one of these buttons is pressed.

DESIGN & BUILD

Smart-pocket build

I found it difficult to come up with a wearable with equipment and sensors that are so big. I was thinking about problems people sometimes have around them. It is quite common for people to put their phones in their pockets while they still have their screen or flashlight on. This is bad for the battery, but also for the phone. Then I came up with the idea: “Smart-pocket”. Smart-pocket indicates when your screen is still on when you put your phone back in your pocket. It also gives a notification when your phone gets too hot, because that is not good for your phone either.

I really liked this assignment myself, because I saw what the options were. That you will receive a notification on your phone in the future when you have left your phone on.
